A personal approach

Progress that feels like your own.

Whether it’s the piano part of a favourite song, a film theme or a classical piece, lessons begin with what excites you, then build the skills to make it happen.

01

Make a clear plan

We’ll define what you want to play and make a practical, enjoyable route to get there.

02

Work through the sticking points

Technique, theory, confidence or coordination, we’ll identify what’s in the way and tackle it together.

03

Understand the music

Alongside playing, you’ll build a real sense of harmony, melody and expression, so music makes more sense in your hands.

About Angus

Music has been central to my life since I was six.

I first fell in love with the piano after hearing the Doctor Who theme played by my first piano teacher. Since then, I’ve followed the music that resonates, from Bach and Bartók to Gershwin, Glass and Reich.

As a performer and composer, I’ve learnt how to listen, create and adapt. That’s what I bring to each lesson: a thoughtful, flexible approach that helps every student find their own spark.

Training & experience

  • MMus with Distinction, Guildhall School of Music
  • First Class BMus (Hons), Royal Birmingham Conservatoire
  • Grade 8 Piano Performance and Grade 5 Music Theory
  • Collaborations include Britten Sinfonia, Bozzini Quartet, Fidelio Trio and the Guildhall Session Orchestra
Frequently asked questions

A few practical details.

Where do you teach?

I travel to students’ homes across North London, including Highgate, Muswell Hill, Crouch End, Archway, Tufnell Park, Kentish Town, Hampstead, Belsize Park, Chalk Farm, Camden, Dartmouth Park, East Finchley, Finchley, Finsbury Park, Gospel Oak, High Barnet, Holloway, Islington, Primrose Hill and Wood Green, as well as nearby areas.

Do you teach other subjects?

Yes. I also teach composition, music theory and music production using Logic Pro and Ableton Live.

Are you DBS checked?

Yes. I hold an enhanced DBS check and am subscribed to the DBS Update Service.

Do you teach grades?

Yes. Grade preparation is available if that is part of your goal.

Do you offer online lessons?

Yes. Online lessons are held on Google Meet.
